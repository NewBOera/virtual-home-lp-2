About Cookies

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit websites. They serve an important role in helping to personalize your online experience and enhance website functionality by remembering your preferences and improving overall site performance.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for core website features including navigation and secure account access.

Analytical Cookies

Help us understand visitor behavior and improve website usability.

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences to provide a customized browsing experience.

Advertising Cookies

Enable the delivery of relevant advertisements and measure their performance.

Consent

By continuing to use TheVirtualDegree, you agree to our use of cookies. While you can adjust cookie settings through your browser, please note that blocking certain cookies may affect how our website functions.

Managing Your Cookies

You have full control over your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, please be aware that disabling specific cookies might restrict your access to certain website features.

Data Privacy and Security

We implement robust security measures to protect any personal information collected through cookies and ensure it is not misused or shared inappropriately.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA regulations, you have the right to:

  • Request information about cookies stored on your device
  • Request corrections or deletion of cookie-related data

Policy Revisions

This Cookie Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes to this policy.
